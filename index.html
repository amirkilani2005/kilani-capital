Rules
Non-negotiable. Every session.
Entry Rules
1
5-min MSS only
Body close above/below swing with displacement. Wicks don't count. If you're debating whether it qualifies, it doesn't.
2
1-min FVG entry only
Drop to 1-min after MSS. FVG in discount (long) or premium (short). No sub-1-min charts. Delete them from your layout.
3
Draw target before entry
Named structural level — previous high/low, equal highs, major FVG. Type it in the journal first. No target = no trade.
4
Minimum 2:1 R/R
Calculate before clicking. Under 2R means skip the trade. Confidence is not a substitute for R/R.
Session Rules
!
Max 2 trades per day
After trade #2 close the platform. Not minimize — close. Trade #3 is net negative across your entire recorded history.
!
1 contract only
For 30 consecutive trading days minimum. Win rate drops from 78% to 33% as contracts increase. Size is earned by process.
!
2 losing days → observe only
Mark setups, record outcomes, do not execute. Reset next session. The market doesn't owe you recovery.
↑
Certainty = warning sign
When you feel absolutely sure, reduce size. Every major blow-up in your history started with certainty.
