Rules & Setups
Non-negotiable. Every session. 10-11 AM EST, NQ only.
Your Two Setups
Setup 1
Continuation Trade
Window: 10-11 EST strictly. Trend continuation model.
Context: Ideally see manipulation in the first 30 min (9:30-10). Not a hard rule — some days are one-directional from open.
Trigger: After 10:00, identify the key liquidity price is drawn toward. Enter on 1m FVG in line with the draw on liquidity.
Target: The identified liquidity pool.
Setup 2
Reversal Trade
Window: 10-11 EST strictly.
Trigger: Sweep of liquidity on 5m chart OR SMT divergence with ES. 1m MSS confirms reversal. Retracement into a 1m FVG in discount (for longs) or premium (for shorts).
SMT: Extra confirmation — not hard-required but presence is never a mistake.
Target: Opposite liquidity.
Session Rules
!
NQ Only · 10-11 EST Only
No other instruments. No other hours. The 9:30-10 window is for observation and context only. You have no edge in the opening 30 min volatility.
!
Minimum 2:1 R/R
Calculate before clicking. Under 2R — skip the trade. Confidence is not a substitute for R/R. The math is non-negotiable.
!
Max 2 Trades Per Day
Hard maximum across both setups combined. After trade #2 close the platform. Not minimize — close.
!
1 Contract Only
For 30 consecutive green days minimum. Size is earned by process, not by confidence. After 30 green days, may scale to 2 contracts.
↑
2 Losing Days → Observe Only
Mark setups, record outcomes, do not execute. Reset next session. The market doesn't owe you recovery. Certainty is a warning sign.
Pre-Trade Checklist
1
Setup Identified
Which setup is this — Continuation or Reversal? If you're unsure, it's not either one. Skip.
2
1m FVG Entry
No sub-1-min charts. Entry only at the FVG. Not before.
3
Target Named
From the structural level list (NWOG, PDH/PDL, 9:30 H/L, LDN/ASIA BSL/SSL, ATH, FVG). Type it before entry. No target = no trade.
4
R/R ≥ 2:1
Verify before click. The calculator shows you live. Below 2R → skip, regardless of conviction.
5
Emotional State ≤ Neutral
Calm or neutral only. FOMO / revenge / forcing / bored → no trade, observe only.
