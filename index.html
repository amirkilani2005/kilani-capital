The Rules
Non-negotiable. Every session.
Entry rules
1
5-min MSS only
Body close above/below swing high/low with visible displacement. Wicks do not count as MSS. If you're debating whether it qualifies, it doesn't.
2
1-min FVG entry only
Drop to 1-min after 5-min MSS. Enter on FVG in discount (long) or premium (short). No sub-1-min charts — ever. Delete them from your layout.
3
Draw target before entry
Named structural level — previous session high/low, equal highs, major FVG. Type it in the journal. No target written = no trade taken.
4
Minimum 2:1 R/R
Calculate before clicking. If planned R is under 2.0, skip the trade regardless of how confident you feel. Confidence is not R/R.
Session rules
!
Max 2 trades per day
After trade #2, close the platform. Not minimize — close. No "one more setup." The data proves trade #3 is net negative across your entire history.
!
1 contract only
For 30 consecutive trading days minimum. Size is earned by process consistency, not rewarded for confidence. Your win rate drops from 78% to 33% as contracts increase.
!
2 losing days → observe only
Mark setups. Record what would have happened. Do not execute. The market doesn't owe you recovery. Reset the next session with a clean slate.
↑
Certainty = warning sign
When you feel absolutely sure about a trade, that is the signal to reduce size — not increase it. Every major blow-up in your journal started with certainty.
