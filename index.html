The Rules
Non-negotiable. Every session.
Entry rules
1
5-min MSS only
Body close above/below swing with displacement. Wicks don't count. If you're debating whether it qualifies, it doesn't.
2
1-min FVG entry only
Drop to 1-min after MSS. Enter on FVG in discount (long) or premium (short). No sub-1-min charts ever.
3
Draw target before entry
Named structural level. Type it in the journal. No target = no trade.
4
Minimum 2:1 R/R
Calculate before clicking. Under 2R = skip regardless of confidence.
Session rules
!
Max 2 trades per day
After trade #2, close the platform. Not minimize — close. Trade #3 is net negative across your entire history.
!
1 contract only
For 30 consecutive trading days minimum. Your win rate drops from 78% to 33% as contracts increase.
!
2 losing days → observe only
Mark setups. Record outcomes. Do not execute. Reset next session.
↑
Certainty = warning sign
When you feel absolutely sure, reduce size. Every blow-up started with certainty.
